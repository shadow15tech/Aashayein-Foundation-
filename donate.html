Donation Guidelines

📄

Donation Receipt

As per Income Tax regulations, the organization provides a donation certificate Form 10BE to eligible donors. For issuing the receipt, donors are requested to share their complete address and a valid government ID.

🇮🇳

Indian Donors

All contributions made by Indian donors to Aashayein Foundation may be eligible for 50% tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

🌍

Foreign Donors

As per the FCRA Act, 2010, foreign donors are requested to share a copy of their passport (front and back pages) before making a donation.

🔒

Secure Donations

All donations are processed through the official bank accounts of the organization, ensuring secure, transparent, and accountable transactions.
